Permafrost Slump Stress Distribution Model

IN PROGRESS. A finite-element model investigating internal stress distributions within retrogressive thaw slumps. Model outputs were compared against field observations to evaluate how slump geometry and headwall angle influence stress concentrations and failure patterns in ice-rich permafrost terrain.
